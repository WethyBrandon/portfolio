Background Information:

ANDORIAN CRUISER
Owner: Andorians
Operator: United Federation of Planets
Type: Carrier
Active: 24th Century

The USS Typhon (NX-85808) was a Federation starship, a Typhon-class mobile carrier fortress in Starfleet service, introduced just after the Dominion War. It was designed to carry 26 fighter class craft, initially Valkyrie-class Federation attack ships. Although it was equipped with full scientific and medical equipment, it was heavily armed with multiple turrets armed with twin mounted quantum torpedo launchers and dual Type-X phasers.

This model was constructed from a piece of concept art. For the texture, I used cuts from the concept art. The ship was designed into seperate pieces and then placed together into one solid mesh. This was the first ship model I ever made in Blender 2.8. It's one of my favorites I have ever created.
