THE GUEST HOUSE

The Guest House is a psychological thriller set in a distant future. The movie starts with a mysterious underground facility and an amnesiac "guest". We follow the "guest" as she tries to live her life the best she can while confined to the corridors of the strangely vacant facility. Once she is on the edge of madness she finds an unlikely companion, a lost little girl that cannot speak. The two continue to try to find a way out and survive. Until a mysterious black-out threatens to seal their fate. Can they solve the mysteries of "The Guest House" and escape?
