STAR TREK: ENTERPRISE ANDORIAN CRUISER: BY BRANDON WETHY

Background Information:

ANDORIAN CRUISER
Owner: Andorians
Operator: Andoria
Type: Cruiser
Active: 22nd Century

The Andorian cruiser was a type of small warship and support craft that was in service with the Andorian Imperial Guard during the 22nd century. During the mid-2150s, a number of these vessels were involved in engagements against the Vulcans and the Romulans.

This model was constructed from a single screenshot from the TV series. For the texture, I wanted to make it as accurate as I could for the on-screen version. The look, dimensions, and scale were all inspired from the shot featured in Enterprise. The final product took about 8 hours to make, which is impressive given the situation that its inception. I made this during an exceptionally long layover in LAX. Pure boredom fueled the creation of this ship.
