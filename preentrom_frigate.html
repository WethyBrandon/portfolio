PRE-ENTERPRISE ROMULAN FRIGATE: BY BRANDON WETHY

Background Information:

PRE-ENT ROMULAN FRIGATE
Owner: Romulans
Operator: Andoria
Type: Cruiser
Active: 22nd Century

This vessel was the epitome of Romulan technology before the Sundering that separated the Vuland and Romulan peoples. One day the re-unification will begin and this ship will be the symbol of Romulan strength.

Originally meant to bridge the gap between Romulan and Vulcan technology, I concepted this ship with the idea “Vulcan, but more violent”. The thought process here was to make a Vulcan-style vessel but with sharp edges to distinguish it from its contemporaries. Vulcans have a mindset of peace and prosperity, but their Romulan cousins only think of war and conquest, thus it must be shown in the differences of their technologies. This Romulan version is more belligerent and ready for battle.
